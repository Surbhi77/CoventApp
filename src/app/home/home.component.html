About our Lab

Designing and scaling life saving resources for all

Only 10% of worldwide expenditure on health research and development is devoted to the problems that primarily affect the poorest 90% of the world's population.

Open icu labs aims to develop a catalogue of low-cost, open source, validated critical care technologies for use in global emerging markets and low resource regions.

We believe that critical care is hard enough even in the best hospitals in the world, and access to a pump, ventilator or oxygen tank shouldn't determine your fate.

CoVent Challenge Corp, our parent 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, is establishing the first open icu lab space in New York City in 2021.

Our Theory of Change

engineers, designers, physicians and humanitarians working together to create economical and validated critical care technology
